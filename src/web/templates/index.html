What it is for

Collect

The IFRC-SRU has been tasked to develop a database that facilitates the consolidation of existing shelter knowledge and build up an inventory of shelter solutions documented in a systematic, methodological manner. Particularly focus is on technical details, construction practices, climatic conditions, availability and suitability of materials and techniques and other critical information that is needed to short track the planning and design of shelter responses.

Save

Confronted with the reality of rising numbers of disasters where sheltering presents a key component of the humanitarian assistance, the Humanitarian Shelter Sector is challenged to deliver more, efficient and sustainable responses in very different contexts. Agencies need to save time and money while implementing safe shelter solutions that will save lives in future disasters.

Share

The collected information is made openly available through the online database. To be efficient and sustainable this documentation effort needs to be shared amongst all actors implementing shelter projects. They are invited to contribute by uploading their shelter solutions so that others can learn from their experience.
