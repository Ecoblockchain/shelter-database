    wall plate (bottom)/ sill plate A horizontal framing element installed on a structure's foundation. The bottom framing member of a wall frame. D 3-3
    wall plate (top) A horizontal wood element attached to the top of a frame wall and supporting trusses, I-joists, rafters, ceiling joists, or other members. D 3-3
services   water supply The distribution of drinking water to consumers by a water utility.  
    wattle Material for making fences or walls, consisting of rods or stakes interlaced with twigs or branches.  
    wattle & daub A type of wall construction comprising interwoven twigs, sticks, and branches (wattle) that are covered with clay, cow dung, or mud (daub). Traditionally used as an infill in post-and-beam timber-frame buildings.  
    welded-wire mesh/ welded wire fabric A square grid of uniformly placed wires, welded at all intersections. Used in concrete slabs or reinforced concrete walls. D 3-1
    wood shingle A roof covering unit made of wood cut into stock sizes and applied on sloping roofs in an overlapping pattern.
